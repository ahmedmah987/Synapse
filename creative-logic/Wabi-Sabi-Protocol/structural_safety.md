# 🏗️ Structural Safety Report

## 1. Material Integrity Testing
Synapse mandated rigorous testing to ensure improvised materials met municipal codes:
- **Debris Composite**: Compressive strength target **≥ 25 MPa** (ASTM C39).
- **Rammed Earth**: Load-bearing capacity **≥ 1.5 tons/m²** (ASTM D1633).
- **Reclaimed Wood**: Treated with borax-based preservatives; 70% tensile strength verified.

## 2. Design Redundancy
- **Lattice Framework**: Recycled steel mesh to distribute loads evenly across earthen walls.
- **Moisture Control**: Integrated gravel trenches and permeable surfaces to prevent waterlogging.

## 3. Risk Mitigation
- **Contaminant Screening**: Portable XRF analyzers used to detect hazardous materials in debris.
- **Fire Resistance**: Clay-based fire-retardant coatings applied to all organic components.
