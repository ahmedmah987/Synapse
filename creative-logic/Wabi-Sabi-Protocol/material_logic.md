# 🧱 Improvised Material Logic

## 1. Material Sourcing Strategy
In response to supply chain disruptions, **Synapse** pivoted to a hyper-local sourcing model:
- **Debris Aggregate**: Broken concrete, scrap metal, and wood fragments from local construction waste.
- **Raw Earth**: Native clay-sand mix excavated directly from the site.

## 2. Processing Techniques
- **Debris Recycling**: Crushed and sieved to create a uniform aggregate, mixed with lime or fly ash.
- **Earth Compaction**: Vibratory compaction to ensure load-bearing capacity and erosion resistance.

## 3. Design Adaptations
- **Modular Joints**: Flexible, biodegradable joints (hemp fiber-reinforced mortar) to accommodate natural movement.
- **Surface Treatment**: Natural pigments (iron oxide, charcoal) for aesthetic protection.
